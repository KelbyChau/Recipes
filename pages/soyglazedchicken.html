Soy Glazed Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams of Skinless, boneless, chicken thighs
  • 2.5 tbsps of honey
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2.5 tbsps of soy sauce
  • 2 tbsps of oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp of onion powder
  • 1 tbsp of paprika
  • 1 tbsp of salt
  • 1 tbsp of black pepper
  • 1 tbsp of oil


Steps:

Prepare the Chicken:

  1. Take your chicken out the packaging and begin to de-skin and bone your chicken thighs, trimming the excess fat off as well.
  2. Weigh the chicken, and season with the onion powder, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Pour the oil into the mixture and massage the seasonings into the chicken.
  4. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
